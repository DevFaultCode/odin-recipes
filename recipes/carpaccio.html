Asian Style Beetroot Carpaccio

Carpaccio is an italian dish of thinly sliced beef meat. So the word carpaccio is used worldwide to name all kinds of raw dishes. So, as you might have guessed, beetroot carpaccio is a salad made with raw beetroot.

The asian style is given by the vinaigrette wich is made with sesame oil and sesame seeds making a sesame vinaigrette


Ingredient:

Beetroot carpaccio

  • medium beet
  • spring onion
  • Black olives

Sesame vinaigrette:

  • Sesame Oil
  • Vinegar
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame seeds
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. To the sesame vinaigrette mix in a bowl the sesame oil, vinegar, soy sauce, semame seeds, salt and pepper

  2. Slice the beetroot with a slicer

  3. put them in the plate

  4. Spread the black olives.

  5. Spread the Sesame vinaigrette.
